Why Bini?

We believe that we can help people make better decisions using the collective wisdom of the Bini network. Our goal is to make comparissons fun and memorable.

About Bini

Bini is defined as someone that is super cool but still means business. We take that to heart, as Bini is a social application that appeals to consumers, as well as retailers. We make socializing, decision-making, and purchasing super-cool!

With the format of compare-and-vote, you can create content through photos, music, and videos, while gathering quick responses, in order to help you make decisions, no matter the size. Dive into our communities, for industry-specific content, as well as discover featured Binis and other unique users.

How to?


Download, sign-up, and complete your profile. Afterwards, the application will show you just how easy it is to discover users by name, inter- est, or hashtags. All it takes is to snap a photo, choose a template, complete a caption, and share. Your friends will see all of your Binis in natural order, so they won’t miss a thing!
